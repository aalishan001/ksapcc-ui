The Karnataka State Action Plan on Climate Change (KSAPCC) aims to combat climate challenges through a multi-faceted approach, focusing on the agricultural and water sectors. To ensure the effective implementation of the KSAPCC, real-time monitoring is vital. Our dashboard facilitates transparency and data-driven decision-making by tracking progress, monitoring resources, and fostering inter-departmental coordination.

Strengthening KSAPCC Monitoring for Effective Implementation

Effective climate action requires strong systems that track progress, highlight gaps, and support timely interventions. The Karnataka State Action Plan on Climate Change (KSAPCC) Monitoring Dashboard brings together data from multiple departments into one unified platform, making it easier to assess implementation, identify bottlenecks, and ensure accountability. By providing integrated reports on outlays, outputs, and outcomes, the system supports policymakers in taking evidence-based decisions and helps Karnataka move closer to its climate goals with greater efficiency and transparency.

Capacity Building for Government Officials

For climate action to succeed, government officials at state and district levels need the skills, systems, and confidence to manage monitoring effectively. The initiative focuses on equipping officers with training in data collection, reporting, and dashboard use, enabling them to contribute actively to the monitoring process. This capacity-building effort promotes smoother inter-departmental coordination, strengthens local-level accountability, and ensures that officials are empowered to implement KSAPCC schemes in a systematic, outcome-oriented manner.

Real-Time Climate Action Monitoring & Reporting

Climate action needs timely insights, not delayed reports. The dashboard enables real-time monitoring of climate-related spending, activities, and outcomes across sectors, helping stakeholders visualize progress as it unfolds. By capturing and displaying data dynamically, the system ensures quick course-corrections, prevents resource leakages, and strengthens accountability. The real-time reporting also connects state action to broader national and global climate commitments, allowing Karnataka to demonstrate leadership in evidence-driven and transparent climate governance.

Citizen Engagement and Feedback Loop

Addressing climate change requires not only government action but also public participation. The dashboard includes a dedicated space for citizen engagement, allowing individuals and civil society to share feedback, track government performance, and contribute ideas. This two-way communication builds trust, enhances transparency, and makes climate governance more inclusive. By encouraging collective ownership, the feedback loop ensures that policies and programmes are more responsive to community needs, ultimately strengthening climate resilience at both local and state levels.

A transparent view into our progress

Our KSAPCC Monitoring Dashboard offers real-time insights, transparent reporting, and strategic tools to drive impactful climate action and build a more resilient Karnataka.
